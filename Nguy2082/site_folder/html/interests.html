Interests

Drawing

When I was a kid, I really like to draw. At first, I drew my favorite anime character, Sailor Moon, and received compliments from my friends when I was six years old. However, I didn't have the chance to learn how to draw. Drawing is still one of my biggest hobbies.






Miniatures

Another thing that I love to do is making miniatures. Little tiny stuffs always take my attention. Spending time doing miniatures make me feel peaceful in my mind.


Photography

Travel is the best way to release stress for me. When I travel to somewhere, instead of taking pictures of me, I want to take pictures of the sea, flowers or sky. Good photos come with good memories.
