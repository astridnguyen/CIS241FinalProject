Home Page

Hello! My name is Hoang Nguyen. You can call me Hoang or Astrid is fine. Black and white are my favorite colors. This is the reason why I chose to use white letters on a black background color for my website. Welcome to my world!
I'm currently attending the DTA program at Edmonds College. My dream is to become a Graphic Designer who can make beautiful artworks. I didn't have any experience in coding or creating a website before. However, web development is more interesting than I thought when I took CIS 241 class. It makes me want to learn more about computer information systems.
In my free time, I love drawing, painting, taking photos, and making miniatures. The decision of being a designer is started from my love of drawing. I want to help people express their feelings into posters, postcards, or pictures. You can visit my interests page to see some of my drawings as well as the miniatures.
Finally, don't forget to sign in to my guestbook and tell me what you think about my website. Thank you!
